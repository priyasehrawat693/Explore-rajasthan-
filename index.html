RAJASTHAN - INTRODUCTION

Rajasthan is India's largest state, famously known as the "Land of Kings". It's renowned for its royal history, magnificent forts, opulent palaces, vast deserts, and vibrant colorful culture.

The capital city Jaipur, also called the Pink City, is part of the famous Golden Triangle tourist circuit along with Delhi and Agra.

From the golden sands of Jaisalmer to the serene lakes of Udaipur, Rajasthan offers a diverse travel experience that transports you to the era of Maharajas.
