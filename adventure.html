DESERT ADVENTURES

Rajasthan ki pehchaan - Experience the golden sands like never before

Most Popular

Camel Safari

Traditional desert exploration on the ship of the desert with breathtaking sunset views over sand dunes.

Locations: Jaisalmer, Bikaner, Pushkar
Experience: Sand dunes par camel ride + sunset view
Duration: 1-2 hours (sunset/sunrise options)
₹800 – ₹1500

Desert Jeep Safari

Fast and thrilling 4x4 ride over sand dunes with adrenaline-pumping drops and turns.

Location: Sam Sand Dunes (Jaisalmer)
Experience: High-speed dune bashing adventure
Group Size: 4-6 people per jeep
₹1500 – ₹3000
Cultural Night

Desert Camping

Overnight cultural experience with folk dance, music, bonfire and traditional Rajasthani dinner under the stars.

Location: Jaisalmer Desert
Experience: Folk dance, music, bonfire + night stay
Includes: Dinner & breakfast, cultural show
₹2500 – ₹6000

Quad Biking (ATV Ride)

Exciting ATV bike riding on sand dunes with professional guidance and safety equipment.

Location: Jaisalmer Desert
Experience: Bike riding on sand dunes
Safety: Helmets and training provided
₹1000 – ₹2000
Wildlife Spotting

Jungle Safari

Explore the wilderness of Rajasthan's national parks in search of tigers, leopards, and other wildlife.

Location: Ranthambore National Park
Experience: Tiger spotting + forest safari
Vehicle: Open gypsy or cantor
₹1500 – ₹3000

Hot Air Balloon Ride

Float peacefully above Rajasthan's landscape with breathtaking views of forts, palaces, and countryside.

Locations: Jaipur, Pushkar
Experience: Forts & palaces ka bird-eye view
Duration: 60-90 minutes flight
₹8000 – ₹12000

Parasailing

Experience the thrill of flying while being towed by a vehicle, enjoying panoramic views from above.

Locations: Pushkar, Jaisalmer
Experience: Rope se bandhe hue sky flight
Height: Up to 150 feet above ground
₹2500 – ₹4000

Paragliding

Free flight experience with a certified instructor, soaring like a bird over Rajasthan's landscapes.

Locations: Jaisalmer, Jaipur outskirts

₹3000 – ₹5000
