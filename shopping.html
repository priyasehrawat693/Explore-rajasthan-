FAMOUS MARKETS OF RAJASTHAN

Explore the most iconic shopping destinations across Rajasthan's cities

Jewelry Hub

Johari Bazaar Jaipur

The jewelry hub of Jaipur, famous for exquisite gold and silver jewelry adorned with precious gemstones. Specializes in traditional Kundan and Meenakari work that reflects royal Rajasthani craftsmanship.

Location: Old Jaipur City
Specialty: Kundan & Meenakari jewelry
Timing: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
₹2,000 – ₹50,000+

Bapu Bazaar Jaipur

Famous for traditional Rajasthani textiles, Bandhani suits, and colorful Mojris (traditional footwear). A one-stop destination for authentic Rajasthani clothing and handicrafts at reasonable prices.

Location: Near Hawa Mahal
Specialty: Bandhani & Mojris
Best for: Traditional clothing
₹300 – ₹3,000

Hathi Pol Bazaar Udaipur

Udaipur's artistic hub famous for miniature paintings, traditional Rajasthani artwork, and wooden handicrafts. The perfect place to buy authentic Mewar-style paintings and home decor items.

Location: City Palace area
Specialty: Miniature paintings
Art Style: Mewar tradition
₹500 – ₹5,000

Rajasthani Shopping Tips & Guide

Smart Shopping in Rajasthan

Make the most of your shopping experience in Rajasthan with these essential tips:

  • Bargaining is Expected: Start at 40-50% of quoted price
  • Best Time to Shop: Morning hours (10 AM - 1 PM)
  • Carry Cash: Many shops don't accept cards
  • Check Authenticity: Look for handmade signs
  • Local Guides: Helpful for finding best shops

Shopping Itinerary Planning

📍 3-Day Shopping Tour

• Day 1: Jaipur (Johari, Bapu, Tripolia Bazaars)

• Day 2: Jodhpur (Clock Tower, Nai Sarak)

• Day 3: Udaipur (Hathi Pol, Bada Bazaar)

• Optional: Jaisalmer for leather items

🛍️ Budget Planning

• Budget: ₹5,000-10,000 (souvenirs)

• Mid-range: ₹10,000-25,000 (with jewelry)

• Luxury: ₹25,000+ (gemstones, carpets)

• Carry extra for shipping costs
