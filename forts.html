Forts & Palaces of Rajasthan

Discover the royal heritage through magnificent forts and opulent palaces showcasing Rajput architecture

Famous Forts & Palaces

Amber Fort (Jaipur)

  • Sheesh Mahal: Mirror palace
  • Diwan-e-Aam: Public Audience Hall
  • Diwan-e-Khas: Private Audience Hall
  • Sukh Niwas: Pleasure palace

Entry Details

Indian Tourists: ₹100

Foreign Tourists: ₹500

Elephant Ride: ₹900–₹1100

Opening Hours: 8:00 AM - 5:30 PM

Mehrangarh Fort (Jodhpur)

  • Moti Mahal: Pearl Palace
  • Sheesh Mahal: Mirror Palace
  • Phool Mahal: Flower Palace
  • Zenana Deodi: Women's quarters

Entry Details

Indian Tourists: ₹100

Foreign Tourists: ₹600

Camera: ₹200

Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Jaisalmer Fort (Golden Fort)

  • Raj Mahal: Royal residence
  • Jain Temples: 7 ancient temples
  • Laxminath Temple: Vishnu & Lakshmi
  • Fort Palace Museum: Royal artifacts

Entry Details

Indian Tourists: ₹50

Foreign Tourists: ₹250

Camera: ₹100

Opening Hours: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Chittorgarh Fort

  • Area: 700 acres
  • Famous For: Rani Padmini's story
  • Vijay Stambh: Victory Tower
  • Kirti Stambh: Tower of Fame

Entry Details

Indian Tourists: ₹100

Foreign Tourists: ₹200

Light Show: ₹150

Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 5:30 PM

Hawa Mahal (Jaipur)

  • Built: 1799 AD
  • Windows: 953 small windows
  • Architecture: Rajput style
  • Purpose: Royal women viewing

Entry Details

Indian Tourists: ₹50

Foreign Tourists: ₹200

Camera: ₹50

Opening Hours: 9:00 AM - 4:30 PM

City Palace (Udaipur)

  • Location: Lake Pichola
  • Size: Largest palace complex
  • Crystal Gallery: Rare crystal items
  • Vintage Car Museum: Royal car collection

Entry Details

Indian Tourists: ₹300

Foreign Tourists: ₹600

Camera: ₹200

Travel Tips & Information

Best Time to Visit

⏰ October to March (pleasant weather)

🌡️ Avoid summer months (April-June)

🌧️ Monsoon season (July-September) has greenery

🎭 Winter months for festivals

Ticket Information

🎫 Book online to avoid queues

📱 Use government apps for tickets

💰 Keep cash for smaller forts

🎟️ Combo tickets available for multiple sites

What to Wear

👟 Comfortable walking shoes

🧢 Hat/cap for sun protection

🧴 Sunscreen and sunglasses

💧 Carry water bottle

Photography Tips

📸 Check camera fees at each site

☀️ Early morning for best light

🌅 Sunset for golden hour photos

🚫 No flash inside palaces
