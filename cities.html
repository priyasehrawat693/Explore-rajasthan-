Eastern Rajasthan (Royal Region)

City Travel Guide & Tips

Planning Your City Tour

Make the most of your Rajasthan city tour with these essential tips:

  • Best Time to Visit: October to March (pleasant weather)
  • Travel Between Cities: Trains & buses well connected
  • Local Transport: Auto-rickshaws, taxis, local buses
  • Stay Duration: 2-3 days per city recommended
  • Cultural Etiquette: Dress modestly, remove shoes at temples

Best Time to Visit Each City

Winter Cities (Oct-Mar)

Jaipur: Pleasant weather, ideal for forts

Jaisalmer: Perfect for desert safaris

Jodhpur: Comfortable for fort visits

Pushkar: Camel Fair (Oct-Nov)

Year-Round Cities

Udaipur: Beautiful in all seasons

Mount Abu: Always cool, monsoon special

Ajmer: Pilgrimage year-round

Chittorgarh: Historical site any time

Summer Retreats

Mount Abu: Only hill station, 20-30°C

Udaipur: Lakes provide relief

Early Morning: Best for desert cities

Heritage Hotels: Often have AC facilities

Rajasthan City Circuit Planning

📍 Golden Triangle Circuit

• Day 1-3: Jaipur (Amber Fort, City Palace)

• Day 4-5: Jodhpur (Mehrangarh Fort)

• Day 6-7: Jaisalmer (Desert Safari)

• Duration: 7-10 days

🏞️ Lake & Romantic Circuit

• Day 1-3: Udaipur (Lake Pichola, City Palace)

• Day 4-5: Mount Abu (hill station)

• Day 6: Ajmer & Pushkar (pilgrimage)

• Duration: 6-8 days

🏰 Heritage & History Circuit

• Day 1-2: Chittorgarh (largest fort)

• Day 3-4: Bikaner (Junagarh Fort)

• Day 5-6: Jaipur (royal heritage)

• Duration: 6-7 days
